FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions.

Straight answers to keep things simple.

Do I need to bring my own hardware?

No. Everything you need for your chosen tier is provided on-site and staged at your reserved bench when you arrive.

Is there an age requirement?

Yes. This is strictly a 21+ event. IDs will be checked at the door before entry. No exceptions.

Are these compatible with my current bowls?

Absolutely. The modular hardware we use is built to standard joint specs, so your current 14mm bowls and bangers will fit perfectly.
